Final Project: Design a System That Monetizes Data

Design a system that turns data into revenue. Your submission must address both the technical architecture and the business model behind it. In your report, clearly explain:
(1) who your target customers are.
(2) how you know this need exists — including your full data-acquisition process (you may build on the methodology from Homework 2) 

(3) how much time, effort, or money the customer would be willing to invest.
(4) Analyzing the difficulties you would face when promoting the product earns bonus points.

Submit a single PDF report (in English) with your GitHub repository link on the first page. Deploying your system online earns additional bonus points.
